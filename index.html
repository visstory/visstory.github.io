The Visual Data Storytelling contest, as part of the IEEE PacificVis conference, celebrates its seventh year in 2023.This contest aims to encourage students, researchers, and visualization practitioners to demonstrate the value of data visualization through compelling visual data stories and to promote innovative and effective use of data visualization for communication and presentation.

This contest explores the emerging data communication genre, including data storytelling, narrative visualizations, explanatory notebooks, and visual essays. Potential contest entrants are encouraged to review the following events and venues for inspiration.
